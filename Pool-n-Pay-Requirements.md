# Pool-n-Pay Product Vision and Requirements

## Purpose
Pool-n-Pay exists to reduce money stress between people who share life moments together.
It should make group money management clear, fair, and emotionally easy.
The product should feel trustworthy, warm, and simple enough for anyone to use.

## Core Vision
Create one complete expense platform that works across web and mobile, where people can:
- Split expenses after spending (traditional flow)
- Pool money before spending (proactive flow)
- Settle quickly with UPI-based payment handoff
- Track personal and group financial activity without confusion

## Product Scope
Pool-n-Pay must launch as:
- A web application
- A mobile application

Both experiences should share the same core product behavior and account identity.
A user should be able to start on web and continue on mobile without friction.

## Primary Users
- Friends traveling together
- Flatmates managing recurring shared costs
- Event organizers and participant groups
- Small social circles that settle frequently
- Individuals who also want personal expense visibility

## User Outcomes
Users should be able to:
- Know who paid, who owes, and who is owed
- Avoid awkward money conversations
- Collect and contribute funds transparently
- See reliable records of every action
- Settle via familiar payment behavior

## Signature Differentiator
Dual-mode group behavior:

1. Split Mode
- Add expenses when they happen
- Split amount across members
- Track balances and settlement status

2. Pool Mode
- Define a target pool amount
- Members contribute upfront
- Group spends from pooled balance
- Remaining balance is visible and manageable

## Must-Have Product Capabilities

### Identity and Access
- Sign up and sign in
- Session continuity across usage
- Clear access control for private group data

### User Profile
Each user profile must support:
- Name and identity details
- UPI ID for receiving money
- Personal settings and preferences
- Personal activity view

### UPI Payment Handoff
- QR-based payment intent generation
- Pre-filled payment details where possible
- Copyable payment link fallback
- Designed for real-world pay-and-confirm behavior

### Group Management
- Create group
- Choose group mode (Split or Pool)
- Add and view members
- Understand group context quickly

### Expense Management
- Add expense with amount and description
- Associate payer
- Select participants for split
- Persist history of group expenses

### Pool Contribution Flow
- Member contributes toward pool
- Contribution visibility and status tracking
- Admin-level approval flow where needed
- Pool balance updates with clarity

### Friends
- Discover/add people
- Send and respond to requests
- Maintain trusted contact network for fast group setup

### Activity Feed
- Timeline of meaningful actions
- Human-readable entries
- Visibility into who did what and when

### Analytics
- Spending overview
- Period-based trend visibility
- Group-level spending comparison
- Useful insights without overload

### Settings
- Personal preference controls
- Notification/reminder intent controls
- Data behavior preferences where applicable

## Experience Requirements
- Calm, premium, beachy/coastal visual identity
- Friendly but clear language around money
- Fast interaction with minimal taps
- Strong mobile-first usability
- Readability in low attention situations

## Trust Requirements
- Every financial action should be understandable
- Statuses should be explicit: pending, approved, settled, disputed
- No silent state changes
- Users should always know next action

## Cross-Platform Product Rules
- Web and mobile must mirror core business behavior
- Differences in layout are acceptable; differences in logic are not
- Profile, groups, balances, and history must stay consistent

## Launch Readiness Definition
The product is launch-ready when a new user can:
1. Sign up
2. Set profile and UPI details
3. Create a group in either mode
4. Add members
5. Add expenses or collect pool contributions
6. Share/scan payment QR for settlement or contribution
7. Confirm and view updated balances
8. Review activity and analytics

## Quality Bar
- Users should not encounter blank states without guidance
- Errors should be actionable and non-technical
- Critical flows should feel stable and predictable
- Money-related records should be easy to audit by a human

## Long-Term Direction
Pool-n-Pay should become the default social money companion for:
- Trips
- Shared homes
- Events
- Recurring informal groups

The brand should stand for:
- Fairness
- Clarity
- Emotional ease around shared money
- Confidence across web and mobile

## Personal Commitment Capture
This product is not just software; it is a meaningful life mission.
The experience should reflect care, dignity, and longevity.
The goal is to ship something that genuinely helps people manage money relationships better.
